FRIEDRICH NIETZSCHE • 1886

Section 37

Identifier: 37 • Part Two: The Free Spirit
Simple modern summary

People hear beyond good and evil and think it just means evil wins now—that is lazy, vulgar thinking. Nietzsche is not doing a simple moral flip between God and devil. He is trying to escape that whole good-versus-evil game.

EXTENSIVE SUMMARY
Context

Basically: section 37 is a witty, sharp rebuke.

Main points
  • Basically: nietzsche refuses to let the will-to-power hypothesis be flattened into a simple moral inversion or Manicha...

Modern companion insight

The same vulgarization happens constantly today — new ideologies are immediately recast as "the new good vs the old evil" or hero/villain narratives. Nietzsche reminds the free spirit to keep the hypothesis experimental and anti-moralistic, refusing to let it become another dogmatic religion of inversion.
