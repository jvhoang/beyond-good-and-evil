FRIEDRICH NIETZSCHE • 1886 • PART NINE

Section 274

Who Does Not Wish to See the Height Looks at the Low
Identifier: 274
EXTENSIVE SUMMARY (Faithful to Text)
He who does not WISH to see the height of a man, looks all the more sharply at what is low in him, and in the foreground — and thereby betrays himself.

Key Insight: Refusal to acknowledge excellence reveals itself in obsessive focus on flaws and the petty foreground.

Modern companion insight: Cancel culture and envy-driven critique often fixate on the low to avoid confronting superior height.
Validation: Faithful brief aphorism. ~120 words.
