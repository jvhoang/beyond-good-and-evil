FRIEDRICH NIETZSCHE • 1886

Section 13

Identifier: 13 • Will to Power, Not Self-Preservation • Part One
Simple modern summary

Life is not mainly about surviving—it is about expressing power and energy. Self-preservation is just a side effect of a deeper drive to grow, act, and push outward. That is what will to power means at its core.
