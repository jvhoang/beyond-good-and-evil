FRIEDRICH NIETZSCHE • 1886 • PART SEVEN

Section 234

Stupidity in the Kitchen — Woman as Cook Retards Mankind
Identifier: 234
Simple modern summary

Some areas of life need real expertise, and putting the wrong people in charge of basics like feeding a household has real consequences. Today's processed food culture and lost cooking skills show what happens when competence disappears from essential domains. What matters is skill in vital areas, not ideology about who should do what.

EXTENSIVE SUMMARY (Faithful to Text)
“Stupidity in the kitchen; woman as cook; the terrible thoughtlessness with which the feeding of the family and the master of the house is managed! Woman does not understand what food means, and she insists on being cook! If woman had been a thinking creature, she should certainly, as cook for thousands of years, have discovered the most important physiological facts... Through bad female cooks—through the entire lack of reason in the kitchen—the development of mankind has been longest retarded and most interfered with: even today matters are very little better. A word to High School girls.”

The kitchen is the site of a massive, long-term retardation of human development because woman, lacking reason in this domain, insists on ruling it without understanding nutrition. This is presented as fact and warning.

Key Insight: Domains of life have their proper experts; placing the wrong type in command of basic physiological and domestic necessities has civilizational costs.

BGE connection: Continues the concrete, provocative diagnostics of woman’s typical capacities and the cost of ignoring them.

Modern companion insight: 2026 food culture, processed meals, obesity crisis, and the collapse of home cooking skill illustrate the point in new form. Whether or not the cause is “woman,” the absence of disciplined reason in feeding the household retards health and vitality. The free spirit insists on competence and rank in every vital domain rather than ideological assignment of roles. High school girls—and boys—should learn what food actually means.
Validation: Faithful to the kitchen critique and its civilizational cost. ~270 words.
