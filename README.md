# Beyond Good and Evil: A Graphical Companion

A professional, modern, and insightful digital companion to Friedrich Nietzsche'
 s *Beyond Good and Evil: Prelude to a Philosophy of the Future* (1886, Walter Kaufmann translation).

## Overview

Friedrich Nietzsche’s *Beyond Good and Evil* is one of the most provocative and rewarding works in Western philosophy. This site offers a clean, engaging, and accurate companion resource designed to help readers follow, remember, and reflect on the text while reading the Kaufmann edition—the standard English translation prized for its precision, readability, and fidelity to Nietzsche’s voice.

For the Preface, every one of the 296 sections, and the Aftersong (“From High Mountains”), the companion provides:
- Concise modern-language summaries faithful to the original arguments and tone
- Highlighted key quotations taken directly from the Kaufmann translation
- Thoughtful thematic analysis and context
- 3–5 original custom Grok Imagine visuals (refined dark-academia style) per presentation

The goal is simple: make Nietzsche’s dense, powerful aphorisms accessible and memorable without replacing the experience of reading the book itself. Ideal for first-time readers, students, teachers, reading groups, and returning explorers.

## Public & Educational Use

This resource is offered freely for personal study, classroom use, lecture preparation, reading groups, and non-commercial educational purposes.

**Embedding instructions:**  
Copy the entire polished `index.html` into a Custom HTML block or embed area on your site. The interactive TOC and cards link out to the live `https://jvhoang.github.io/beyond-good-and-evil/sections/XXX.html` pages (images load from the public `/images/` folder). The single-file design renders beautifully and reliably across devices and embed contexts.

**Attribution:** When referencing or quoting the original text, please cite Walter Kaufmann’s translation. This companion is an independent educational aid and is not a substitute for reading Nietzsche’s work.
